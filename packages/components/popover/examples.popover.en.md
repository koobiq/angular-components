#### Hiding on scroll

By default, the popover hides when its trigger scrolls out of its scroll container. Set `[hideIfNotInViewPort]="false"` to keep it open.

<!-- example(popover-hide-on-scroll) -->

#### Custom scroll strategy

Override `KBQ_POPOVER_SCROLL_STRATEGY` to change how the overlay reacts to scrolling (e.g. `reposition` instead of the default hide-on-scroll).
